
In order to distil a life philosophy, i think it is important to first inventorize and collate that which i already know incl. the origins of my philosophy and the components which make up its body.  

My philosophy will be constructed out of these building blocks:
- the litany against fear [[1]](https://dune.fandom.com/wiki/Litany_Against_Fear#Content_of_the_Litany) 
- стойкий мужик [[2]](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rRSIPvuYwOY)
- non fui, fui , non sum, non curo 
- not everything that is faced can be changed but nothing can be changed until it is faced

the above suggests that it can be best modelled by epicureanism [[3]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Epicureanism) which emphasises “[...] freedom from pain and fear”.  

Additional aphorisms that influence me are:

- such is life
- it is what it is
- there is no joy without pain
- [...] for endeavour is its own reward    
- if it is humanly possible, you can do it    
- how you do something is how you do anything    
- life is not a mystery to be solved but a reality to be experienced    
- don’t think. just do.    
- don’t be afraid to be afraid.    
- it takes courage not to be discouraged.    

Possible distillation: Accept fear to act courageously. Overcome fear to act rationally. If ever in doubt, act because nothing can be changed until it is faced. If faced and found to be immutable, let go.
